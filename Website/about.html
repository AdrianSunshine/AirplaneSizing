Our Project

The Airplane Sizing Project is an initiative aimed at developing methods for determining the optimal size and dimensions of airplanes.

Our Team

We are a team of aviation enthusiasts, students and young-engineers passionate about pushing the boundaries of aerospace engineering. Our diverse backgrounds and expertise allow us to approach airplane sizing from multiple angles, ensuring well-rounded and innovative results.
